Soft City is a publishing imprint and printing press focusing on artist's books, zines, and editions by emerging artists, founded in 2014 in Brooklyn, NY.

Risograph Printing

Soft City prints on a risograph that allows for any papersize up to 11in x 17 inches. We have an array of colors and types of papers. We have printed for artists books and singular prints. Contact us for inquiries about your print job.
